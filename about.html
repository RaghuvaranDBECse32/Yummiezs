About Us

Meet the Creator

Hi! I’m Sharmila, a Computer Science Engineering graduate with a passion for creating simple, user-friendly, and low-cost applications to solve everyday problems.

The Yummy Food App was born out of my love for cooking and the idea of making recipes more accessible to everyone, regardless of budget. This app is designed to provide users with easy-to-follow recipes for appetizers, entrées, desserts, and beverages—all with a fresh and fun user experience.

Our Mission

In today’s fast-paced world, cooking can feel like a luxury or a chore. Our mission is to help people rediscover the joy of cooking by providing curated recipes that are affordable, easy to make, and delicious.

Whether you're cooking for yourself or hosting friends and family, the Yummy Food App is here to make your culinary journey stress-free and enjoyable.
